# 📜 Narration in Games: Lost Unity
*Dynamic level adaptation and procedural narrative design.*

The **Narration in Games** course focused on the intersection of story-driven concepts and technical implementation. My group developed "Lost Unity," a third-person adventure RPG designed for PC.

### 🎮 The Concept:
**Lost Unity** is set in an open world divided into procedural island levels. The core innovation lies in its **Dynamic Narrative Adaptation**:

*   **Responsive World:** Unlike traditional branching paths, the "tube levels" of each island adapt physically to the player's previous choices and sequences.
*   **Replayability:** Every playthrough offers a unique structural game world based on the player's moral and tactical decisions.
*   **Atmosphere:** A photo-realistic graphic style designed for deep immersion in a world being purged of evil.
