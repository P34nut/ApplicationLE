# 🎮 Internship: Daedalic Entertainment
*Quality Assurance Intern | Sep 2016 – Feb 2017*

During my Bachelor's studies, I completed a six-month internship at **Daedalic Entertainment** in Hamburg, one of Germany's most renowned adventure game developers and publishers.

### 🔍 Responsibilities & Impact:
*   **Quality Assurance:** Conducted extensive manual testing across multiple titles to ensure stability, gameplay balance, and technical polish.
*   **Controller Testing:** Specialized in verifying controller support and input mapping for console ports and PC versions, ensuring a seamless player experience across different hardware.
*   **Bug Reporting:** Documented and tracked issues using industry-standard tools, collaborating closely with developers to reproduce and resolve critical bugs.

### 🕹️ Supported Titles:
*   **Shadow Tactics: Blades of the Shogun** – Hardcore tactical stealth game.
*   **State of Mind** – Cyberpunk thriller focused on transhumanism.
*   **Oh My Gore!** – A subversion of the tower defense genre.
*   **Leaves – The Journey & Leaves – The Return** – Artistic point-and-click puzzle games.
